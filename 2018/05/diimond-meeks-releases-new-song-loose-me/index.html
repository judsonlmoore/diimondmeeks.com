DIIMOND MEEKS RELEASES NEW SONG “LOOSE ME” FT. CEELO GREEN AND ACCOMPANYING VIDEO

by Judson L Moore | May 21, 2018 | Press Releases | 0 comments

World Premiere at Sayers Club Hollywood Last Thursday

Watch the Official “Loose Me” video here

LOS ANGELES, CA – May 21, 2018 – DIIMOND MEEKS released his new song “Loose Me” feat. CeeLo Green along with the accompanying video via Mothership Entertainment / RED MUSIC. Co-produced by CeeLo Green, “Loose Me” is available for streaming / purchase now, watch the “Loose Me” video HERE.

On Thursday evening in the stylish and swaggering Sayers Club in Hollywood, RED MUSIC artist DIIMOND MEEKS belted the lyrics at his world premiere of his new single, “Loose Me”, to a packed audience of fans, fellow label artists, and celebrity guests. Friends of the artist including “Big Hero 6” Star Jamie Chung and “How to Make It in America” beau Bryan Greenberg, “Typical Psycho” singer Katie Welch and “The Magicians” Lead Andrew James Allen were among the many who showed up to support Meeks, whose performance jolted the room and became an instant fan favorite. Accompanied by a stunning music video, curtain drops and fog machines added to the theatrics that took backstage to Meeks symphonies and vocal range. Following DIIMOND’s performance, Greenberg shared the stage with a performance celebrating the artist.

DIIMOND MEEKS, formerly known as Dezmond Meeks, is an award winning producer, songwriter, actor, dancer and multi-instrumentalist, known for his over the top stage presence, flamboyant style and broad five octave vocal range. His unique sound, fusing pop, rock, soul, funk and EDM, landed him a deal with Sony Music/Mothership Entertainment (CeeLo Green). Born in Louisiana, he made his national television debut in 2005 as a contestant on the 4th season of American Idol and again in 2011 on the 6th season of Americas Got Talent, ahead of his debut full length album “Cinderfella” in 2012. DIIMOND MEEKS his first official single for Sony Music’s RED MUSIC, “Loose Me” feat. CeeLo Green, on Friday, May 18th. The track, also co-produced by CeeLo Green, is his first release under his official new name as DIIMOND MEEKS.

DIIMOND MEEKS is an electrifying award winning recording artist signed to Sony Music Entertainment after a chance meeting with Grammy Award Winning Artist Ceelo Green. His edgy androgynous fashion, showmanship and jaw dropping five octave vocal range established him among industry elite. In 2017 he began working on his debut album with some of the worlds most innovative and legendary producers.

His debut single “Loose Me” coproduced by and featuring Ceelo Green was released on May 18, 2018. After headlining residencies across Los Angeles, London and Paris, DIIMOND moved to New York in the Spring of 2018 to further career opportunities in the recording industry. On June 1, 2018 iHeartRadio picked “Loose Me” as one of their playlist selections for Pride Month and added Meeks as a guest curator.
